THE NATIONAL COAT OF ARM

Nigeria information

The national coat of arm is an art work designed to show the solidility of the country,it was designed to help defined the country in a unique way

It consist of

  1. The Eagle
  2. The two white horses
  3. The Y shape

The Eagle
It represents the strength of the nation
The two white horses
It represents Peace and Unity
The Y shape
It represents the two main rivers in nigeria; River Benue and Niger
